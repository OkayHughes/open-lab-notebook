Idea:

  • p≡psp\equiv p_sp≡ps​ and ϕ≡ϕs\phi \equiv \phi_sϕ≡ϕs​ indicate that we should start initialization at the surface
  • Assume that we know physical pressure ppp and geometric height zzz.
  • The eta coordinates used for initialization give us dp3d\textrm{dp3d}dp3d.
  • The EOS states that if we know Δϕ\Delta \phiΔϕ, then we can calculate p=−RdT⋅dp3dr^2Δϕ. p = -\frac{R_d T \cdot \textrm{dp3d}}{\hat{r}^2 \Delta \phi}.p=−r^2ΔϕRd​T⋅dp3d​.
  • Therefore a consistent initialization results from progressively rootfinding from the lowest model level to the top such that the EOS is satisfied.
